#dogsarebarking

CAMPAIGN

DOGS: Is an old expression for having sore feet.

We believe that everything deserves comfort. Inspired by regrets of high heel wearing on a night out, ShoeEaze was birthed from the yearning for comfort whilst our ‘dogs were barking’. Due to the amazing reviews we’ve received about saving your dogs on a night out, we decided to give back 10% of our sales to local charities supporting the care of abandoned dogs.
